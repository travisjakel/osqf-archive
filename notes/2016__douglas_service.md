## Key findings
- The Luxor strategy's expected log returns are derived in continuous time using geometric Brownian motion.  
  > derive the Luxor closed form expected log returns in a continuous time setting
- Exponential filtering of the logarithm of asset prices enables analytical tractability.  
  > gain analytical tractability by using the natural logarithm of the price
- The difference indicator corresponds to the MACD oscillator in technical analysis.  
  > known as moving average convergence divergence (MACD) oscillator
- Position entry/exit rules are based on sign changes in the difference indicator.  
  > Position indicator transitions... enter a long position

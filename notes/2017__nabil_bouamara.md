## Key findings
- Majority of UCITS funds exhibit zero-alpha after peer performance evaluation  
  > Decomposition: majority are zero-alpha funds (Barras et al.; 2010)
- Peer performance evaluation reduces alpha by 37% through downward correction  
  > Downward correction 37 percent.
- Risk-adjusted performance improves after measurement error correction  
  > Significant risk-adjusted performance when correcting for measurement error.

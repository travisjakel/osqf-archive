## Key findings
- R-Ladies mission emphasizes creating safe spaces for women and non-binary individuals in R community.  
  > 'R-Ladies isn't a separate R users group for women. It's an R users group and it's organized by women.'
- Code of Conduct ensures harassment-free environments across all R-Ladies spaces.  
  > R-Ladies is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone.
- Diversity initiatives include hiring diverse teams and encouraging representation in conferences.  
  > Hire more diverse individuals in your respective teams to bring all minority folks into this field.

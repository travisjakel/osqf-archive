## Key findings
- DFC outperforms DCC in modeling correlated volatility shocks  
  > DFC model is related to other multivariate GARCH models: ∀t, hv,t = 1: Special case of Bollerslev's (1990) CCC
- TVV and VIN both carry distinct risk premiums  
  > Univariate sorts for loadings on VIN and TVV show average return spreads between 3.31% and 1.62% per year
- Correlated volatility innovations explain cross-sectional return variation  
  > VIN and TVV are both priced in the cross-section of equity returns

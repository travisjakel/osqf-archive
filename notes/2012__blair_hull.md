## Key findings
- Speed is critical in trading, with HFT firms leveraging queue position advantages post-Reg NMS.  
  > If you have a mouse in your hand you are too late.
- HFT profits surged after Reg NMS implementation in 2007.  
  > There is some evidence that HFT profits took a large jump in 2007 after Reg NMS was implemented
- Flash Crash was directly linked to current market structure, not just Waddell and Reed.  
  > Our results show the Flash Crash can be linked directly to current market structure

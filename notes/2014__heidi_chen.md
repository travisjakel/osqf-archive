## Key findings
- The package implements the ISDA Standard Model for CDS valuation  
  > The ISDA Standard Model Standardized coupon, recovery rate, and first accrual dates Conversion between conventional spread and upfront payments
- The package provides detailed CDS contract analysis with risk metrics  
  > > summary(cds1) Contract Type: SNAC TDate: 2014-04-22 Entity Name: Xerox RED: 9HC533 Currency: USD End Date: 2019-06-20 Spread: 105.8 Coupon: 100 Upfront: 1,862 Spread DV01: 483 IR DV01: -0.74 Rec Risk (1 pct): -2.12
- The package calculates IR DV01 and recovery risk metrics  
  > IR DVO1: -0.74 Rec Risk (1 pct): -2.12

## Key findings
- Regulatory requirements like Fed SR 11-7 mandate thorough model documentation to the 'Nth degree' for auditability.  
  > Fed SR 11-7:Supervisory Guidance on Model Risk Management
- The simple FX forward formula V = e^(-rT)(F - K) × N requires rigorous definition of variables (r, F, K, N, V) for accurate pricing.  
  > V = e −rT (F − K ) × N
- Consistent pricing across desks for identical OTC contracts is challenged by differing interpretations of risk sensitivities and currency denominations.  
  > identical contracts on different desks should be priced identically

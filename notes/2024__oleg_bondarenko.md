## Key findings
- MMI hypothesis works for GBP but fails for EUR due to tick size changes  
  > MMI Fails Spectacularly for EUR Low R², Two distinct Clusters
- TIH coefficients align with theoretical expectations (βIL ≈ 0.5, βTC ≈ 2)  
  > Beta coefficients (βIL, βTC) are close to (0.5, 2)?
- GBP results align with prior S&P E-mini findings under MMI  
  > MMI seemingly works well for GBP Consistent with results for S&P E-mini in ABKO (2018)

## Key findings
- Crowd-sourced sentiment analysis can detect suspicious activity without traditional SARs  
  > Investors have an interest in monitoring suspicious activity. Let's let them help us do our due diligence.
- Penny stocks show unique ML/TF vulnerabilities through volume-price anomalies  
  > PENNY STOCKS & MONEY LAUNDERING SET PARAMETERS - SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY INDEX VOLUME PRICE VOLUME/PRICE ANOMALY
- OTC markets enable money laundering through unregulated transaction channels  
  > Many vulnerabilities can be located in the OTC market and through electronic intermediaries where there is no one to report.

## Applications
- enhancing AML surveillance
- detecting suspicious activities in penny stocks
- improving SAR compliance

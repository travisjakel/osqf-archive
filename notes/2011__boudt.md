# Intraday Liquidity Dynamics Of The DJIA Around Price Jumps

_2011 · Boudt (Lessius - K.U.Leuven)_

**Headline:** Analyzes intraday liquidity changes in the DJIA around price jumps using NYSE TAQ data and statistical models.

## Key findings
- Price jumps lead to persistent volatility increases (30+ min)  
  > Persistent increase in volatility [30 min], some anticipation
- Spreads widen significantly after jumps  
  > Increase in spreads [30 min], some anticipation
- Volume increases sharply and remains elevated (>60 min)  
  > Sharp and persistent increase in volume [> 60 min], anticipation
- Depth shifts to aggressive side but dissipates quickly  
  > Increase in depth: goes to the most aggressive side [6min], but dissipates quickly

## Key findings
- R and LATEX provide rock-solid foundations for literate slides with consistent math and design.  
  > R & LATEX: Rock-solid, still going strong with roots in the 1970s for a reason
- kableExtra requires manual HTML/LATEX formatting in 10% of cases.  
  > kableExtra: Docs: “Only have to mess with raw HTML/LATEX in the last 10%
- R.rsp allows mixing R with any language but has poor error messages.  
  > R.rsp: Error messages tend to defy interpretation…
- A grammar of tables is needed for cell merging, formatting rules, and operator notation.  
  > Grammar of tables: Vocabulary for assembling common tables… operator notation to assemble things

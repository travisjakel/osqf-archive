_2019 · Jingyu He (Chicago Booth)_

## Key findings
- HEL outperforms traditional methods (MA, EW, OLS) in portfolio performance across 10/30/49 industry classifications.  
  > HEL achieves 5.8x SPY in 2018 vs 2.7x for OLS.
- Macro predictors (long-term yield, inflation) and firm characteristics (dividend yield, accrual) show joint significance.  
  > Significant predictors include long-term yield, inflation, stock market variance, dividend yield, accrual, and gross profit.
- Bayesian SUR framework enables joint prediction of returns and covariance matrices for asset allocation.  
  > Bayesian modeling is powerful for asset allocation as it provides predictive covariance matrices.

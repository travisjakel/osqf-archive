_2014 · Vyacheslav Arbuzov (Perm State National Research University, Master in Finance & Information Technology program)_

**Headline:** Analysis of HFT regulation impacts, order book dynamics, and structural market changes using Russian and EU market data.

## Key findings
- Russian tax implementation reduced order-to-trade ratios by ~50% (from 100,000:1 to 50,000:1)  
  > Time | Order to trade ratio
03.09.2012 | 100000
- Cancellation ratio dropped from 0.975 to 0.930 post-tax implementation  
  > Time | Cancellation ratio
03.12.2012 | 0.930
- Russian market showed similar price distribution patterns to LSE 2001

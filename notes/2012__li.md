# Monetary Policy Analysis Based On Lasso-Assisted Vector Autoregression (LAVAR

_2012 · Li (University of Notre Dame)_

**Headline:** This talk presents Lasso-Assisted Vector Autoregression (LAVAR) for analyzing monetary policy impacts using high-dimensional data.

## Key findings
- LAVAR outperforms FAVAR in prediction accuracy with fewer parameters  
  > Fewer nonzero parameters, Simple model, Better predictions
- Complex models may lead to suboptimal predictions  
  > Be cautious when fitting complex models
- LAVAR selects economically meaningful variables  
  > AVG HOURLY EARNINGS: 0.85 (LAVAR) vs 1.05 (FAVAR)

## Applications
- forecast short-term interest rates
- forecast yield curves
- assess monetary policy effects
- predict bond/equity risk premia

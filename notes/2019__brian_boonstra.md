## Key findings
- Cryptotoken returns are fundamentally fatter-tailed than equity returns  
  > Cryptotoken returns (in USD terms) are fundamentally fatter-tailed than equity returns
- Bitcoin returns are fatter-tailed than Ethereum returns  
  > Bitcoin returns are fatter-tailed than Ethereum returns
- Classic market-making is unprofitable due to tiny spreads  
  > Combined with tiny spreads, this means classic market-making is unprofitable
- Fatter tails not explained by stochastic volatility  
  > The fatter tails are not explained by (slow-moving) stochastic volatility

_2025 · Kris Boudt (Dutch Central Bank (Annick van Ool))_

**Headline:** Analysis of Dutch pension fund annual reports to assess adherence to the Prudent Person Rule through text analysis of investment policy, risk management, governance, and sustainability disclosures.

## Key findings
- All four PPR pillars received increasing attention from 2003-2023, with governance rising most in 2003-2012 and sustainability in 2013-2023  
  > 2003-2023: All four pillars of the PPR have received more attention • First decade (2003-2012): Biggest increase in attention for how the company organizes its governance
- ABP showed systematically higher attention to investment policy and sustainability compared to PFZW in recent years  
  > ABP attached systematically more attention to investment policy in the last four years • Consistent with that, ABP attaches substantially more attention to sustainability in the last three years
- No statistically significant effects found between PPR communication levels and future fund returns  
  > No Effects of Prudent Person Rule Talk on Future Return

## Key findings
- Restricted MIDAS with normalized exponential Almon lags outperformed AR(1) and U-MIDAS in MSE ratios for IBC-Br data  
  > restricted MIDAS with a normalized exponential Almon lag polynomial beats the benchmark when employing IBC-Br
- IBC-Br provided statistically superior MIDAS specifications via DM-test  
  > only the IBC-BR provides a statistically superior MIDAS specification (DM-test)
- Restricted MIDAS weighting scheme showed decay after 12th lag with minimal parameter proliferation  
  > Most weights peak on the first lags and then decay smoothly with almost no mass after the 12th lag

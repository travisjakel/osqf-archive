## Key findings
- BCP method increases returns while reducing market exposure  
  > Returns kept by the BCP method are increased and at the same time reduce the exposure to the market.
- BCP identifies changing market dynamics through posterior probabilities  
  > The posterior probability (~p ) describes the probability that the dynamics (µ) have changed.
- BCP results show improved mean returns with lower volatility  
  > The G10 carry trade returns have a mean of 31 basis points... sd = −0.023

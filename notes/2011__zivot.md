## Key findings
- Factor models explain 75% of portfolio returns in the FoHF example  
  > R2 of factor model for portfolio ≈ 75%
- FMMC uses full sample distributions for factors and unequal history for assets  
  > Use full data for factors and unequal history for assets
- Euler’s theorem enables additive risk decomposition for factors and portfolio weights  
  > SD, VaR and ETL are linearly homogenous functions
- Tail risk measures like VaR/ETL require non-parametric estimation  
  > Estimate tail risk...non-parametrically from simulated return data
- Hedge fund risk contributions vary by strategy type  
  > 3 long-short equity (LS-E), 3 event driven...

## Key findings
- Over-reliance on backtesting leads to failure due to confirmation bias and poor out-of-sample performance.  
  > 1. Source and explore some bad data.
2. Find confirmation of your preconceived notion.
3. Back test all the things.
4. Sort by PNL.
- Strategy focus is critical; most failures stem from neglecting this.  
  > Focus on the strategy (the #1 thing people fail to do)...
- Predictive power, not speed, is key to successful trading.  
  > '...the quantitative predictive aspect is the key to success. Just trading fast doesn't make you any money if it is the wrong trade.'
- HFT profitability requires tiny edges (e.g., $0.0006/share) and extreme efficiency.  
  > HFT Profitability (Tradeworx): 5% market share... $0.0006/share edge

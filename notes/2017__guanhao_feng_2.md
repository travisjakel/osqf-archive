## Key findings
- A method for calibrating real-time market odds using the Skellam process was presented.  
  > We provide a method for calibrating real-time market odds for the evolution of score difference for a soccer game.
- The Skellam process models score differences with parameters derived from market odds.  
  > N(t) = N A(t) - N B(t) = W A(t) - W B(t) ~ Skellam(λ A t , λ B t ),
- Market odds imply probabilities that can be compared to Skellam-distribution forecasts.  
  > Market Prob. (%) 21.93 vs Skellam Prob.(%) 19.50 for score difference 0

## Key findings
- Intraday trading invariance holds with β = 0.671 in OLS regression, close to the theoretical 2/3 slope.  
  > log N vs log W, Three Regimes... Solid line: n t = c + 0.671 · w t
- Invariance fails during the Flash Crash's first 8-12 minutes but holds otherwise.  
  > Invariance operative until last minute of Crash period - Fails during first 8-12 minutes following Crash.
- Trade size deviations occur at regime transitions (lower at openings, higher at closings).  
  > Regime Opening: Trade Size lower than predicted... Regime Closing: Trade Size higher than predicted

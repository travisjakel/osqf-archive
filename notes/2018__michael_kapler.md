## Key findings
- Cryptocurrency seasonality patterns show significant variation across months (e.g., BTC-USD November 2010-2017 had 0.5% average returns)  
  > BTC-USD Avg Nov 2010 - 2017 | BTC-USD Avg Nov 2017 | Top 25% / Bot 25%
- SPY shows consistent positive seasonality in January (avg 0.7% in Jan 2000-2018)  
  > SPY Avg Jan 2000 - 2018 | SPY Jan 2018 | Top 25% / Bot 25%
- Avoiding spring/summer months can reduce drawdowns in adaptive allocation strategies  
  > Avoid spring and summer to reduce drawdowns.

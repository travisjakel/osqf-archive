## Key findings
- Linear relationships between assets and hedging instruments simplify risk management (e.g., futures hedging).  
  > If the relationship between Y and X is linear, then the risk management (RM) problem is simple
- Non-linear relationships require ongoing monitoring and adaptive strategies (e.g., options hedging).  
  > what is good today is not necessarily good tomorrow
- Complex models risk loss of interpretability in risk management frameworks.  
  > In complex system, such mechanisms can be vague/cumbersome, loss of interpretability

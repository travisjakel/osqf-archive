# Matrix Profile Enhanced Bayesian Online Change Point Detection for Bitcoin ETF Trading

_2025 · Masoud Neshastehriz (Institute for Financial Services Analytics Alfred Lerner College of Business & Economics University of Delaware)_

**Headline:** Developing an explainable online workflow combining Bayesian Online Change Point Detection (BOCPD) and Matrix Profile Analysis (MPA) to detect distributional shifts and recurring regimes in Bitcoin ETF trading.

## Key findings
- SPY shows more frequent parameter shifts than IBIT but with smaller magnitudes.  
  > SPY shows more frequent change points, reflecting a more dynamically evolving return process
- IBIT and SPY have similar regime coverage (33% vs 35%) despite differing market dynamics.  
  > Regime Coverage: Are we often in a regime?... IBIT 33%... SPY 35%
- A simple regime-based trading strategy for IBIT outperformed buy-and-hold in Q3-Q4 2024.  
  > Q3 41%... Q4 49%... Buy-and-Hold Return 0.05%... 47%

# Multi-Factor Models and Analytics with R, OneTick, and OneQuantData

_2014 · Maria Belianina_

**Headline:** Demonstration of PCA-based multi-factor trading strategies using OneTick's time series database and R for real-time/historical financial data analysis.

## Key findings
- PCA can filter portfolio noise to generate predictive trading signals  
  > Claim: By filtering out 'noise' in a portoflio of stocks through PCA, we might be able to 'predict the future' and make some gains
- OneTick enables real-time and historical data analysis for trading strategies  
  > OneTick price and quote history. Portfolio of stocks. Test 1 day at a time.
- R integration allows statistical analysis of sliding sub-matrices  
  > Pass sliding sub-matrices into R for PCA, etc

## Applications
- high frequency trading signal generation

- pre/post-trade TCA

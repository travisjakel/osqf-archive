# The Impact of News Readability on Market Response Times

_2011 · Switanek (Kellogg School of Management)_

**Headline:** Analyzes how news readability affects how quickly markets react to news events

## Topics
- news readability
- market response times
- financial news analysis
- information processing in markets

## Methods
- random sampling of firms and news articles
- Lee-Ready algorithm for trade classification
- Flesch Kincaid Grade Level for readability measurement

## Datasets used
- random sample of 1000 publicly traded firms
- 320,000 articles and press releases from 1994 through 2010

## Code & tools
- Lee-Ready algorithm
- Flesch Kincaid Grade Level

## Key findings
- News readability significantly influences market response times  
  > Estimated Survival by Flesch Kincaid Grade Level
- Equity market news has the fastest median response time (120 minutes)  
  > Time to first response (min) 120
- Readability metrics correlate with survival probabilities of news effects  
  > Junior High 1.0 High School 1.20 College 1.14 Masters 1.09 Doctorate 1.09

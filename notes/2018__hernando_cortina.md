# The Out-of-Sample Alpha of Just Stocks

_2018 · Hernando Cortina (JUST Capital)_

**Headline:** The Out-of-Sample Alpha of Just Stocks demonstrates how companies with high ESG rankings generate higher returns and lower risk compared to low-ranked peers.

## Key findings
- Top JUST score-quintile stocks showed 14% higher annualized return and 7% lower volatility than bottom-quintile stocks.  
  > Top JUST score-quintile stocks exhibited 14% higher annualized return over the period, 7% lower volatility, and 11% lower betas than bottom-quintile stocks.
- Controlling for Fama-French factors, top quintile stocks generated 3.5% annual out-of-sample alpha.  
  > Fama-French regression alphas: +3.5% for Q5 and -7.1% for Q1 per year
- Strong worker pay & benefits scores reduce stock volatility and drawdowns.  
  > Strong worker pay & benefits and worker treatment scores significantly reduce stock volatility and drawdowns.

## Key findings
- Bayesian Hierarchical model improves forecasting accuracy for ROE and revenue growth across horizons  
  > Higher accuracy from Bayesian Hierarchical model driven by firm level persistence ratios across short and long horizons, for both ROE and revenue growth.
- Firm-level persistence ratios outperform uniform persistence in duration factor returns  
  > BH offers firm level persistence ratio and generate higher factor return than uniform persistence ratio from cross-sectional pooled model
- Post-GFC underperformance linked to broken mean-reversion effect  
  > The underperformance of duration factor during post GFC period is strongly linked to the broken mean-reversion effect.

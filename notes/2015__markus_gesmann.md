## Key findings
- Lloyd’s uses data warehousing and management information systems to track syndicate performance  
  > Submission: Syndicate submit data to Lloyd’s via CMR‡; Storage: Market data is stored centrally in DWH†
- Benchmarking helps identify top and bottom performing syndicates  
  > Top performing syndicates sit in the top right quadrant; bottom performers in the bottom left quadrant
- Visual tools like the 'Whale' chart highlight profit opportunities  
  > Cumulative Net U/W Profit (£m) chart shows potential profit vs actual profit by syndicate

## Key findings
- News-based factors (e.g., recession attention) show statistically significant predictive power for portfolio returns  
  > Recession attention -8.207*** (1.686)
- Economic sentiment has moderate explanatory power but higher noise compared to attention-based metrics  
  > Economic sentiment 0.330 (0.475)
- News-based indicators can approximate traditional macro indicators with greater timeliness  
  > News-based sentiment is highly correlated to the results of the survey while being more timely.

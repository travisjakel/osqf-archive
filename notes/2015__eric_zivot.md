# Price Discovery Share-An Order Invariant Measure of Price Discovery with Application to Exchange-Traded Funds

_2015 · Eric Zivot (University of Washington)_

**Headline:** Introduces a new order-invariant price discovery measure (PDS) for ETFs, showing it outperforms Hasbrouck's IS in simulations and reveals SPY's dominant role in price discovery over IVV, especially during market stress.

## Key findings
- PDS provides accurate estimates of price discovery shares compared to IS's wide confidence intervals  
  > PDS gives accurate estimate
- SPY contributes more to price discovery than IVV, with asymmetry increasing during abnormal trading  
  > SPY is found to contribute more... during abnormal trading periods
- PDS is order-invariant and equivalent to IS when covariance matrix is diagonal  
  > Equivalent to IS when Σ is diagonal

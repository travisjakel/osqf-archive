## Key findings
- Current PE modeling relies on primitive methods like DCF and roll-your-own spreadsheets without fee integration.  
  > GPs: originators use DCF models and report a modeled NAV... does not account for fees
- The PE package provides first comprehensive risk management framework for PE funds.  
  > Outline the first comprehensive risk-management framework... become the standard model
- Model includes continuous-time cash-flow dynamics and fee structure analysis.  
  > We introduce a continuous-time model for cash-flow and value dynamics... evaluate their impact

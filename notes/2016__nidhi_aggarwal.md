## Key findings
- Algorithmic trading (AT) improves market quality by reducing quoted spreads and increasing depth  
  > AT is good for market quality, but a) no significant impact on the depth though, b) no evidence in support of increase in flash crashes
- Co-location adoption led to measurable improvements in market quality metrics  
  > Estimation using a Difference-in-Difference regression with matched securities and matched dates.
- No evidence found that AT increases frequency of large price swings (2%/5%/10% excess moves)

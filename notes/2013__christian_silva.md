## Key findings
- Toy models show good agreement with real data  
  > Good agreement with data
- Moving averages are generally not optimal in stationary markets  
  > In a stationary world MA is generally not optimal (largest Sharpe)
- MA's primary purpose in non-stationary markets is trend estimation  
  > In a non-stationary reality, the main goal of MA is to be an estimation of the local asset drift (trend)

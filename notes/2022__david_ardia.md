## Key findings
- Standard models (CAPM, Fama-French, Carhart) explain little of hedge fund returns  
  > The standard factors explain little of hedge fund returns As a result, the estimated industry performance is high
- New factors (variance, carry, TS momentum) reduce average alpha to -0.2% per year  
  > Average alpha turns negative at -0.2% per year Only 50% of the funds deliver positive alphas
- Misspecification increases estimation noise due to omitted factors affecting all funds  
  > Large realizations of the omitted factors drive the residuals of all funds simultaneously These realizations are not diversified away

## Applications
- improving hedge fund performance evaluation
- identifying relevant factors for hedge funds
- model selection under misspecification

## Key findings
- The evolution of tools from Lotus 1-2-3 to Excel to R was driven by data size limits and academic influence.  
  > Evolution : Lotus 1-2-3 -> Excel -> R
- R's adoption improved version control, testing, and collaboration between quants and traders.  
  > People realized that moving from a spreadsheet to a "real" language had the benefits we all know: version control, testing, maintainability, etc.
- Trading desks use R for predicting client behavior through price patterns.  
  > It is about predicting human behavior through the traces they leave in numerical patterns of security prices

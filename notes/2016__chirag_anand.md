# Liquidity provision in a high-frequency environment

_2016 · Chirag Anand (Macro/Finance Group, NIPFP)_

**Headline:** Analyzes liquidity provision dynamics between algorithmic traders and non-AT in Indian equity markets using NSE order data.

## Key findings
- Algorithmic traders (AT) account for 90.23% of order cancellations but only 8% of trades.  
  > AT contribute significantly to order cancellations (90.23%), but have a little share in trades (8%).
- Most order cancellations occur within 1 second and are not at the touch price.  
  > Significant percentage of order cancellations within one second (63%). Most of these ‘fast’ cancellations are away from the touch.
- Fleeting orders are not a significant portion of market activity.  
  > We do not see significant percentage of fleeting orders.

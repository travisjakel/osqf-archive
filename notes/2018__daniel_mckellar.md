## Key findings
- Geography is the dominant factor in information flow despite globalization.  
  > Geography appears the dominant factor in information flow, despite globalization & low communication costs
- Country membership explains community structure better than sector/industry.  
  > In all cases, countries most closely describe community structure
- Information connections increase return correlations beyond country/sector effects.  
  > Information connections are related to higher return correlations, after controlling for country and sector commonalities

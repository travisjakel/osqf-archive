# Do Mutual Funds Exploit Information from Option Prices for Equity Investment?

_2016 · Hsiu-lang Chen (University of Illinois at Chicago)_

**Headline:** The study examines whether mutual funds use options price information to gain an edge in equity investments, finding evidence of exploitation in fund holdings.

## Key findings
- Mutual funds achieve higher 4-factor alpha using options information in fund holdings compared to CRSP universe stocks.  
  > The 4-factor alpha on the hedge portfolio (D10 - D1) for stocks in the CRSP universe is 61 bps. It is stronger for stocks in fund holdings.
- Funds with higher VW-Spread quintiles generate 33 bps alpha over 3 months using options information.  
  > The 4-factor alpha based on the net-of-expense fund returns for the hedge portfolio (Q5-Q1) is 33 bps.

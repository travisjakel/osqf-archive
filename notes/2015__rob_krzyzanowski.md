## Key findings
- Data scientists spend 50-80% of time on data preparation.  
  > "Data scientists spend 50 to 80 percent of their time collecting and preparing digital data."
- Advanced models are rarely used in production due to deployment friction.  
  > Result - Advanced and complicated models are rarely used in production.
- Data preparation must be trained, not just modeled.  
  > Identical mathematical operations require different logic in train versus predict.
- R frameworks enable zero-code deployment of complex pipelines.  
  > Our most complex models went from 1,000s of lines of code to 100.

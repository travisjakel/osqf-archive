## Key findings
- Spreads in markets arise from adverse selection due to asymmetric information.  
  > Basic idea: Spreads exist due to adverse selection.
- Price series remains a martingale despite order imbalances.  
  > price series is a martingale.
- Simulations show bid-ask spreads narrow over time as market makers infer asset value.

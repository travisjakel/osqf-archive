# How Common is Insider Trading? Evidence from the Options Market

_2023 · Oleg Bondarenko (University of Illinois at Chicago)_

**Headline:** The study finds that insider trading in options markets declined significantly after Raj Rajaratnam's 2009 arrest, evidenced by the loss of predictive power in option trading strategies.

## Key findings
- Option trading strategies lost predictive power after Raj Rajaratnam's 2009 arrest.  
  > Long-short strategy in stocks based on open-buy put-call ratio... Alpha, bps -4.35 post-Oct-2009
- Put-call ratio predictability vanished coinciding with insider trading enforcement.  
  > the timing coincides perfectly... explains why CBOE and ISE signals lost predictive ability at once
- PC ratio profitability exceeded other stock anomalies by 6 bps/month.  
  > Only 2 out 154 anomalies have |t-stat| > 2... PC ratio’s profitability is large relative to other stock anomalies

## Key findings
- Stepwise correlation outperforms singlepass correlation in portfolio returns.  
  > Stepwise Correlation has room to work... stepwise correlation outperforms singlepass correlation.
- Momentum drives returns, while volatility improves risk-adjusted returns.  
  > Momentum drives returns, factoring in volatility helps, stepwise correlation outperforms singlepass correlation.
- Stepwise correlation benefits smaller portfolios but dilutes with more assets.  
  > Its effects dilute when more securities added in to wash out idiosyncratic risk.

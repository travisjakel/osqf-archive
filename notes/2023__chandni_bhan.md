## Key findings
- Hybrid ML models outperformed buy-and-hold strategies with 37.3% total return (2015-2022)  
  > A backtest from 2015 to 2022 showed that strategies from both models significantly outperformed the buy & hold strategy.
- Machine learning detected recoveries faster than rule-based models  
  > The machine learning model tends to pick up recoveries quicker than the rule-based model.
- EWI dashboard uses 130+ variables across asset classes  
  > 130+ variables across asset classes to identify risk-off and risk-on environments

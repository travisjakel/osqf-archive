## Key findings
- Institutional ownership is positively correlated with both return volatility and market microstructure noise.  
  > The results suggest that institutional /mutual funds ownership are positively correlated with both return volatility and noise.
- Mutual fund ownership shows a stronger positive relationship with volatility compared to institutional ownership.  
  > MfOwn 0.091 24.729*** (coefficient magnitude higher than InstOwn's 0.030 14.408***)
- Daily Amihud measure is negatively correlated with volatility/noise in high-frequency data.  
  > The Amihud Ratios in our study are negatively related with the volatility and the noise.

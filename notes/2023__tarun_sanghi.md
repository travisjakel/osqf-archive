## Key findings
- Low credit risk securities exhibit strong 12-month mean reversion  
  > Strong mean reversion among low credit risk securities within top/bot ValMo deciles over 12 months
- High credit risk securities show enhanced 1-month momentum  
  > Enhanced momentum among high credit risk securities over 1 month
- CCR-ValMo interaction terms improve return forecasts  
  > CCR-ValMo interaction terms add-value in forecasting forward 1 month and 12 months excess-returns
- Combined model achieves 86.4% rating prediction accuracy

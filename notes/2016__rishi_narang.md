## Key findings
- Most quant funds claiming ML use are actually just applying linear regression  
  > so much of what is being paraded about as 'ML' is in practice just linear regression
- ML success in alpha models is rare despite hype  
  > almost no one utilizing ML is successful... especially in the alpha model itself
- Signal separation and algorithm validation are major challenges  
  > very difficult to separate signal from noise... prove your algorithm is doing what you meant

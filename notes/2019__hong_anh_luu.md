## Key findings
- Food inflation volatility was 2.45% with 5% quantile -4.1% and 95% quantile 3.8%  
  > Volatility = 2.45%

5% quantile = -4.1%

95% quantile = 3.8%
- Cereals contributed 26% to unconditional food volatility  
  > Contributions of components in unconditional food volatility

Cereals: 0.26
- Cereals showed highest correlation (0.71) with overall food inflation  
  > Correlation       Meat           Dairy         Cereals         Oils          Sugar
Food           0.53           0.57           0.71           0.63           0.43

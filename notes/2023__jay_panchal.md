## Key findings
- qicR enables R users to interface with quantum computing frameworks like Qiskit and QuTip  
  > Integration QuTip: Target to apply measurement/density operators to prepare for VQE
- VQE shows promise for portfolio optimization despite current hardware limitations  
  > QC application in prototype/demo stage, limitation on hardware even with VQE algorithm
- QAE provides quadratic speedup for Monte Carlo option pricing simulations  
  > Quantum Amplitude Estimator (QAE) - Speedup when sampling from distributions for simulation
- Adiabatic systems like D-Wave excel in financial optimization problems  
  > Nota Bene: The adiabatic/annealing solution from D-Wave here has significantly higher success

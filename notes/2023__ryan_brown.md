## Key findings
- Carbon intensity sensitivities show low correlation with emissions data (Pearson 0.26)  
  > Correlations: Pearson Spearman Carbon intensity 0.26 0.12
- WACI sensitivity has high negative correlation with carbon intensity (-0.03)  
  > WACI sensitivity -0.03 0.44 0.43
- Completion portfolios exhibit significant bias (std dev/forecasted risk >1)  
  > The reference red line is one, reflecting an unbiased measure of risk.
- Carbon proxies have low cross-sectional R-squared (0.11-0.29)  
  > cross-sectional R-squared is on the vertical axis, for each set of variables

## Key findings
- State space models enable flexible time series analysis with latent states and observation equations.  
  > State space models provide a very general way to think about time series modeling.
- The parameter W in the state equation critically affects volatility dynamics.  
  > Notice the importance of W !!!!
- Bayesian inference via MCMC is used to estimate latent states and parameters.

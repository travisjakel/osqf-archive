## Key findings
- MDFA avoids explicit data interpolation by applying fractional shifts to low-frequency data.  
  > No explicit data-interpolation: ‘fractional’ shift applied to low-frequency data
- High-frequency real-time estimates are achieved by tracking targets on the high-frequency scale.  
  > Target is tracked on the high-frequency scale to obtain a high-frequency real-time estimate
- System of indicators detects recession signals through sub-series draw-downs and lead/lag patterns.  
  > System of indicators: draw-downs observed in all indicators

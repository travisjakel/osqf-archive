## Key findings
- fedz1 consolidates over 20,000 Z.1 datasets into a single accessible format  
  > The project is a web scrapping that brings more than twenty thousand datasets into one place.
- The package includes functions for exploring series definitions and table descriptions  
  > users can utilize functions to explore tables, definitions, and the meaning of each series.
- Example use cases include analyzing household Treasury purchases during monetary policy changes  
  > The household purchase of Treasuries increased significantly during the tightening monetary policy.

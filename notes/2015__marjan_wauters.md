## Key findings
- Dynamic style portfolios achieve higher Sharpe ratios than single-characteristic portfolios.  
  > MVDSγ=5: 10.06% mean, 13.99% vol, 0.44** Sharpe
- Dynamic allocation reduces estimation risk and improves out-of-sample performance.  
  > Imposing constraints reduces the estimation risk and can improve the portfolio performance (e.g., Jagannathan and Ma, 2003)

# Quantifying Company Impact on the UN Sustainable Development Goals

_2019 · Rochelle March (S&P Global Market Intelligence)_

## Key findings
- Trucost's tool identified $233 billion in SDG-aligned revenues across 13 pilot companies (87% of total revenues)  
  > we uncovered $233 billion of SDG-aligned business revenues - equivalent to 87% of total revenues generated by the companies.
- SDG risk exposure analysis incorporates 45 SDG-related metrics across 189 countries and 26 industry sectors  
  > We then extended the matrix by 45 SDG-related metrics, and did this for each stage of the value chain.
- Company SDG evaluations combine positive impact scores (0-100) with risk mitigation efforts  
  > The SDG Evaluation metric ranges from 0-100 and represents the balance between a company's positive impact and risk exposure associated with the SDGs

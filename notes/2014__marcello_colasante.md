## Key findings
- Factor Entropy Pooling addresses qualitative views (e.g., higher price/earnings implies higher returns) by combining prior distributions with views through relative entropy minimization.  
  > What is the optimal investment strategy if we believe that, qualitatively, higher price on earnings imply higher returns, but we do not know precisely?
- Under normal assumptions, FEP analytically updates expected returns and covariances while maintaining CAPM equilibrium constraints.  
  > Posterior distribution (analytical solution) µ = µ + σ²a'(aσ²a')⁻¹(ξ − aµ) σ² = σ² + σ²c'[...]
- Numerical FEP with low-rank-diagonal covariance matrices reduces computational complexity from O(n³) to O(k³) where k ≪ n.  
  > Small number n(k + 2) of parameters (k ≪ n) [...] binomial inverse theorem
- Back-testing reversal strategies using FEP shows Sharpe ratio improvements vs standard approaches that fix volatilities and Sharpe ratios.
